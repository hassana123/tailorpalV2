## Getting Started

### What Can the Voice Assistant Do?

The TailorPal voice assistant is designed to help you:
- Add new customers
- Record body measurements
- Create and track tailoring orders
- Update order statuses
- Delete customers
- Ask questions about your shop
- Correct mistakes without restarting

### Two Ways to Access the Voice Assistant

1. **Floating Voice Button** - Small microphone icon on your screen
   - Click it anytime to start speaking
   - Perfect for quick commands
   
2. **Full Voice Assistant** - Dedicated voice page
   - Opens in a full dialog
   - Best for longer interactions

### How It Works

1. Click the microphone icon
2. Speak clearly (like you would to a friend)
3. The assistant listens and responds
4. Follow the prompts to complete your task

## How to Use the Voice Assistant

### Speaking Clearly

The voice assistant understands natural speech, but here are tips for best results:

- **Speak naturally** - You don't need to be formal. Talk like you normally do.
- **One thing at a time** - If you're adding a customer, just focus on that task.
- **Take pauses** - Let the assistant finish responding before you speak again.
- **No need to shout** - Normal speaking voice works great.

### Understanding the Responses

The assistant will:
- **Ask clear questions** - "What is the customer's email?"
- **Confirm information** - "Got John Smith. Is that correct?"
- **Guide you through steps** - One step at a time, never overwhelming
- **Suggest next actions** - "You have 5 pending orders. Want to update them?"

### Conversation Flow

The assistant remembers what you're working on, so:
- You can ask related questions mid-flow
- If the assistant asks for something, just answer it naturally
- You can go back and fix things if needed

## Managing Customers

### Adding a New Customer

**Say:** "Add a customer" or "Create a new customer"

The assistant will ask for:
1. **First name** (required) - "What is the customer's first name?"
2. **Last name** (optional) - "What's the last name?"
3. **Email** (optional) - "What's their email?"
4. **Phone** (optional) - "What's their phone number?"
5. **Address** (optional) - "What's the address?"
6. **City** (optional) - "Which city?"
7. **Country** (optional) - "Which country?"
8. **Notes** (optional) - "Any extra notes?"

**Tips:**
- You can skip any field by saying "skip"
- You can provide multiple details at once: "John Smith from New York, email john@example.com"
- The assistant will confirm everything before saving

**Example conversation:**
```
You: "Add a customer"
Assistant: "What is the customer's first name?"
You: "John Smith from New York"
Assistant: "First name: John, Last name: Smith. Email?"
You: "john@example.com"
Assistant: "Got it. Phone number?"
You: "555-123-4567"
Assistant: "Ready to save John Smith. Confirm?"
You: "Yes"
Assistant: "Done! John Smith added successfully."
```

### Finding a Customer

**Say:** "Find customer [name]" or "Look up [name]"

The assistant will search for the customer and show you their details.

### Listing All Customers

**Say:** "List customers" or "Show all customers"

The assistant will show your recent customers.

### Deleting a Customer

**Say:** "Delete customer" or "Remove [name]"

The assistant will ask you to confirm before deleting.

## Recording Measurements

### Starting Measurement Recording

**Say:** "Add measurements" or "Record measurements for [customer name]"

The assistant will guide you through:
1. **Select customer** - Who are you measuring?
2. **Select measurements** - Which measurements do you need?
   - Standard measurements (chest, waist, sleeve, etc.)
   - Custom measurements (anything specific)
3. **Enter values** - The assistant asks for each measurement
4. **Review and save** - Confirm everything before saving

### Standard Measurements

These are common measurements in tailoring:
- **Chest** - Around the fullest part of the chest
- **Waist** - Around the natural waist
- **Hip** - Around the fullest part of the hips
- **Sleeve Length** - From shoulder to wrist
- **Shoulder Width** - Across the shoulders
- **Inseam** - Inside leg length
- **And more...** - The app shows all available options

### Custom Measurements

You can add any custom measurement:
- Example: "Collar size", "Pocket placement", "Hem length"
- Just say the measurement name and value

### Example Conversation

```
You: "Record measurements"
Assistant: "Which customer? Say a name from your list."
You: "John Smith"
Assistant: "Select measurements from the popup. You can also say names like 'chest, waist'."
You: "Chest and waist"
Assistant: "Chest measurement in centimeters?"
You: "40"
Assistant: "Got chest: 40cm. Is that correct?"
You: "Yes"
Assistant: "Waist measurement?"
You: "32"
Assistant: "Done! Ready to save measurements. Confirm?"
You: "Yes"
Assistant: "Measurements saved for John Smith."
```

### Correcting Measurements on the Fly

You can correct values without restarting:
```
You: "40"
Assistant: "Chest: 40cm. Correct?"
You: "No, it's 42"
Assistant: "Changed chest to 42cm. Correct?"
You: "Yes"
```

## Creating & Tracking Orders

### Creating a New Order

**Say:** "Create an order" or "New order for [customer name]"

The assistant will ask for:
1. **Customer** - Who is this order for?
2. **Description** - What are we making? (e.g., "Men's suit")
3. **Fabric Details** (optional) - What fabric/material?
4. **Due Date** (optional) - When should it be ready?
5. **Total Price** (optional) - How much is the order?

### Updating Order Status

**Say:** "Update order status" or "Change order status"

The assistant will ask:
1. **Which order** - Which customer's order?
2. **New status** - What's the new status?
   - pending
   - in_progress
   - completed
   - delivered
   - cancelled

### Viewing Orders

**Say:** "List orders" or "Show my orders"

Shows all your orders.

**Say:** "Pending orders" or "Orders in progress"

Shows only orders that aren't finished yet.

## Correcting Mistakes

One of the smartest features of TailorPal's voice assistant is the ability to correct mistakes **without restarting**.

### How to Correct

You can say:
- **"No"** - If the assistant gets something wrong
- **"Wrong"** - If a value isn't right
- **"Correct that"** - To fix something
- **"Change it to..."** - To provide a new value
- **"Actually, [correct value]"** - To provide the right information

### Examples

**Correcting a name:**
```
Assistant: "First name: John?"
You: "No, it's Jane"
Assistant: "Changed first name to Jane. Correct?"
You: "Yes"
```

**Correcting a measurement:**
```
Assistant: "Chest measurement: 40cm. Correct?"
You: "No, actually 42"
Assistant: "Changed chest to 42cm. Correct?"
You: "Yes"
```

**Correcting mid-conversation:**
```
You: "John Smith, 555-1234"
Assistant: "Got John Smith, phone 555-1234. Email?"
You: "Wait, the phone is wrong. It's 555-4321"
Assistant: "Got it. Changed phone to 555-4321. Email?"
```

### Important Notes

- You can correct at **any time** - Don't feel locked into an answer
- Just say what needs to change
- The assistant will **always confirm** before saving
- No need to start over!

## Tips & Tricks

### Working More Efficiently

1. **Provide multiple pieces of info at once**
   - Instead of: "John" then "Smith" then "john@email.com"
   - Try: "John Smith, john@email.com"
   - The assistant is smart enough to separate details!

2. **Use customer names naturally**
   - The assistant recognizes names you've used before
   - Just say their name and the assistant remembers

3. **Context aware**
   - If you're working with an order, say "Update status" and the assistant knows which order
   - You don't need to repeat information

4. **Batch operations**
   - Working through multiple measurements? Keep going - the assistant stays focused
   - Moving between customers? Just say the new name

### Common Patterns

**Quick customer addition with measurements:**
```
You: "Add customer Jane Doe"
You: (provides info)
You: "Yes, add measurements now"
(Continues to measurement flow)
```

**Updating multiple order statuses:**
```
You: "Update order status"
You: "John's order"
You: "Completed"
Assistant: (confirms)
You: "Update another"
You: "Sarah's order"
You: "In progress"
```

### Working with the Floating Assistant

- **Quick questions** - Use the floating button for 1-2 sentence interactions
- **Longer tasks** - Open the full assistant for complete flows
- **Back and forth** - The floating assistant works great for natural conversation

## Troubleshooting

### "I said something but the assistant didn't understand"

**What to do:**
- Speak a bit slower
- Make sure there's no loud noise in background
- Try saying it differently
- The assistant learns from you!

**Example:**
```
You: "Add customer"
Assistant: (didn't catch it)
You: "Create a new customer" (say it differently)
```

### "The microphone isn't working"

**Check:**
1. Is your microphone enabled in your browser? (Check permissions)
2. Is another app using the microphone?
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Try a different browser

**For permissions:**
- Look for the microphone icon in your browser's address bar
- Click "Allow" if asked
- Or check your browser settings

### "The voice assistant gave wrong information"

**You can always correct it:**
- Use the correction system (see [Correcting Mistakes](#correcting-mistakes))
- Say "No" and provide the correct information
- No need to restart!

### "I want to stop speaking"

**Just say:**
- "Cancel"
- "Never mind"
- "Stop"
- "Start over"

The assistant will stop and reset.

### "The assistant is being too slow"

**This rarely happens, but if it does:**
- Make sure you're connected to the internet
- Try refreshing the page
- The first command might be slower as things load

## General Questions

### Can the voice assistant answer questions not related to my shop?

**Yes!** The voice assistant is intelligent and can answer:
- General knowledge questions
- Business advice
- Questions about tailoring
- How-to questions
- And much more!

It's like having a smart assistant that also knows how to help run your tailor shop.

### What happens to my voice recordings?

Your voice is converted to text immediately and processed locally. We don't store your voice recordings. Your privacy is important to us.

### Can I use voice assistant on mobile?

**Yes!** The voice assistant works on:
- Phones (iOS and Android)
- Tablets
- Computers
- Any device with a microphone and web browser

### How accurate is the voice recognition?

The voice assistant uses advanced speech recognition and gets smarter as it's used. It understands:
- Natural speech patterns
- Different accents
- Variations in how you speak
- Context from your shop

**It's not perfect**, but it's designed to be forgiving and allow corrections at any time.

### Can multiple people use the voice assistant?

Yes, each person who logs in gets their own conversation history and context. The assistant remembers what each person is working on.

### What if the internet connection drops?

You'll get a notification and can try the command again. Your session is saved, so you can pick up where you left off.
